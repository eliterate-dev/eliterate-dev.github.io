##### Hardware

<div class="sticker" style="rotate:-12deg;">✨</div>

Sometimes I like to dabble with microprocessors and IoT stuff too. I've worked with Arduinos and I've done a lot of stuff with various forms of Raspberry Pis, from Pi-holes to hosting Minecraft servers and Discord bots.

My favourite piece of hardware is just the Raspberry Pi pico though. More of an Esp32 style microcontroller than their other SBC offerings, but simple and a lot of fun to tinker with. I use them for silly things like making LED strips flash in pretty colors.

I'm not very good at soldring, but I make do.

I have all the components ready for an Raspberry Pi powered Arcade cabinet, but I've put that on the back burner for now until my main software project is done.

#### Stuff I like

##### Travel

When I'm not typing away on my keyboard all night, I like to travel to faraway places.

I've been all over Europe having had the luck to be born in the middle of it.

My ex was from South Africa, so I have been there a few times too. Beautiful country, though I only know the area around Johannesburg, really.

In recent years I have mostly been to the US for travel. 2024 and 2025 I attended <a href="https://opensauce.com/">Open Sauce</a> in San Francisco, which to be honest blows through most of my travel budget for the year.

I don't regeret it though. It's a super fun event with <a href="https://opensauce.com/featured-creators/">lots of super interesting people</a>. Anyone who has the time and money to do so should absolutely come.

I plan to go again this year if family circumstances allow it.
